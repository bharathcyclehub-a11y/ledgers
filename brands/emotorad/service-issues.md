# EMotorad — Service / Warranty / Quality Issues

Primary source: **`BCH_x_EMotorad_Service_Review.pptx`** (20 slides) — BCH's own audit of the WhatsApp group *"Bch X EM service team"*, prepared 12 Jun 2026. Covers **24 Feb 2024 → 12 Jun 2026**.
Reconstructed verbatim from: **8,820 messages · 280+ ticket numbers · 711 follow-up chases · 1,020 evidence photos.**
Plus live escalations from the Sandeep/Jaya chats.

## Headline numbers (the brand's own record telling on itself)
- **Average resolution 8 days** vs the **48-hour promise** (April-2025 audit: 50 tickets, 37 unclosed).
- **711 chase messages** BCH had to send over 28 months (peak **225 in Q3-2025**).
- Worst cases: **88-day throttle (#81618), 80-day motor (#84001), 55-day replacement cycles (#89002/4)** — up to **44× the promised TAT**.
- **6 of 16** issues from the last 3 months are **still open or unclear** as of 12 Jun 2026.
- **Root cause found Sep-2025**: EM agents' Zoho ticket comments were set to **"private"** — invisible to BCH for ~2 years, which is why nothing moved without chasing.

## Six recurring categories
1. **Product quality** — motors, batteries, displays, brakes, frames; DOA units & whole defective lots.
2. **Spare-parts delay** — replacements take days→months, stock-outs with no ETA.
3. **Communication delay** — days of silence; BCH chases for updates/LR/ticket status.
4. **Logistics failure** — lost shipments, untraceable LRs, disputed PODs, transit damage.
5. **Warranty dispute** — claims denied on contested grounds; verbal commitments walked back.
6. **Process / ticketing** — no ticket numbers, premature "closed – no response", wrong parts shipped.

## Systemic / recurring product-quality flags
- **Mechanical brake system** — repeated pad/caliper failures across Ranger, X1, Viper → BCH asked for **root-cause investigation** (still open).
- Out-of-box / DOA defects across 2024–26: bent forks (transit), damaged seats in sealed boxes, damaged/missing throttles, missing battery/charger/toolkit, frame paint defects, **whole T-Rex+ lot missing headset spacers (Sep-2025) — unsellable**.
- Replacement spares themselves arriving defective/damaged (frames Aug-24, gears leaking grease Oct-Nov-25).
- Battery warranty contested over a **"tampered sticker"**; display warranty found to be **6 months only** at point of denial.

## What service costs BCH (from Syed's 10-Mar-26 message to Sandeep)
- **1,800+ free services** done — neither client nor brand paid.
- ~**30–40 complaints/month**; store resolves 10–15 in-house (tech Sravan).
- Every delivery ≈ ₹700; Desire-series accessory kit ≈ ₹1,000 — absorbed by BCH.

## BCH's 6 recommendations to EM (from the deck — the "asks")
1. Real pre-dispatch inspection (signed PDI photos per unit) + treat repeat defects as **batch** issues + root-cause the brake system.
2. **Regional spare stock** (Bangalore/Hyderabad depot) — *promised Aug-2024, still pending*; service dispatches exempt from audit freezes; let dealer fix small items against a **monthly credit note**.
3. Written **SLAs with auto-escalation** (4h first response, 48h dispatch decision, 72h resolution).
4. **LR at dispatch, POD at delivery** — never close on courier status alone.
5. Published **warranty matrix** + one-time evidence checklist; honour meeting commitments.
6. Every issue gets a ticket; **nothing closes without dealer consent**; quarterly joint Zoho audit + shared scorecard.

**The one fix that matters most (deck's conclusion): take the dealer out of the follow-up loop** — nothing moves until BCH chases; everything moves once leadership (Sandeep/CEO) is tagged.
