4 · How to keep this going (your SOP)

Rule: discount lives on the invoice, never on the payment.

1. New sale invoice arrives → add a row to the Discount Register: Bill No, Invoice amount, Taxable = Invoice ÷ 1.12 (use the actual taxable from the GST invoice if items aren't all 12% GST), Discount = Taxable × 18%. Mark status PENDING, voucher DISC-PEND-<billno>.
2. Payments stay round figures — record them as-is against the running balance. Do not try to attach discount to a payment.
3. When Lucifer issues a credit note (DIS-xx) → find that bill's row, change status PENDING → Credited (DIS-xx), and note any short/excess vs the 18% figure.
4. Always report two numbers: Confirmed balance (matches Lucifer) and True net (after pending discounts). The gap = what you're still owed in discounts.
